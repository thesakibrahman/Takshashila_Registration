SCHOLARSHIP TEST

AN INITIATIVE BY

TAKSHASHILA SCHOOL OF CIVIL SERVICES

Key details

  • Program Name: UPSC/APSC Civil Services Examination coaching.
  • Selection Process: The selection of candidates will be based on their performance in an offline Written Test.

Eligibility criteria

  • Candidates must be residents of Assam.
  • Candidates must be a 10+2 in any stream from a recognised college/ university.
  • Candidates must have a valid email ID.

Details of the test

  • Mode of the test: offline.
  • The test shall be of two-hour duration.
  • Question format: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) with four options
  • Total questions: 100 - All questions shall be compulsory. There shall be no choice.
  • Marks for a correct answer: 2 marks
  • Penalty for wrong answers: 1/3 of marks for the correct answer shall be deducted.
  • No negative marking for questions not attempted.
  • Date : 03rd March, 2024
  • Time : 11:00 AM to 01:00 PM
  • Venue- Madhabdev University, Narayanpur
  • Last date for registration- 29th February, 2024
  • Registration mode: Online
  • Registration Fees- 100 Rs Only
  • Candidates must bring original valid ID proof (PAN Card/ Aadhaar Card/ Voter ID/Driving License etc.) on the examination date.
  • After registration for the Scholarship test, once the payment is done, candidates can download their Admit Card.
  • Selection will be purely on merit basis.

Aspiring candidates who wish to join this coaching program should prepare for the written test and ensure they meet any eligibility criteria that may be set by the program organizers. It's a valuable opportunity for those looking to excel in the UPSC Civil Services Examination. Good luck to all the prospective candidates!
