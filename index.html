Important instructions for filling out the registration form:

  • The candidate must carefully read the instructions before entering or selecting options. The candidate should provide all the required details while completing the online form. Mandatory fields are marked with an asterisk (*) sign.
  • The filling of the online application consists of two parts.
  • In part I, the candidates will have to pay the registration fee through the QR code available here
  • In Part II registration, the candidate will have to fill in basic personal information. Upon submission of the details, the candidate will be prompted to check the information and make corrections, if necessary, in the application.
  • After this stage, no further corrections or modifications will be allowed in Part II.
  • The application is considered incomplete without payment, uploading of a scanned photograph, and signature.

Part II of the application form consists of the following stages:

  • Uploading of photograph and signature etc.
  • Once all the modules (Payment details, Photo, and Signature) are finalized, click "Final submit" to complete the online application process.
  • Once the application is verified by the admin, candidates can download their admit card. Candidates are requested to check the portal after 48 hours
